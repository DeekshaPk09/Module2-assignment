Covid 19/Corona Virus

Symptoms

Symptoms include fever, dry cough and tiredness aches and pains sore throat diarrhoea conjunctivitis headache loss of taste or smell a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes. Serious symptoms include difficulty breathing or shortness of breath chest pain or pressure loss of speech or movement.

Treatment

If you feel sick you should rest, drink plenty of fluid, and eat nutritious food. Self-isolate from other family members and contact your medical provider or a COVID-19 information line for advice. Seek medical care if you have a fever, a cough, and difficulty breathing. Call in advance.

Prevention
